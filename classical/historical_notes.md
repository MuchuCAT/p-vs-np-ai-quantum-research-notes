# Historical notes (P vs NP)

This file keeps a short, citation-backed timeline.
It is intentionally lightweight.

---

## 1970s: foundations
- 1971: Cook introduces NP-completeness via SAT (Cook–Levin theorem). `[Coo71]`
- 1972: Karp gives a list of NP-complete problems via reductions. `[Kar72]`
- 1975: Relativization barrier (Baker–Gill–Solovay). `[BGS75]`
- 1979: Garey–Johnson textbook consolidates NP-completeness. `[GJ79]`
- 1979: Cook–Reckhow introduce propositional proof systems. `[CR79]`

## 1980s–1990s: lower bounds and barriers
- 1981/1982: Random oracle evidence separating P and NP with probability 1 (Bennett–Gill). `[BG81]`
- 1984: AC0 lower bounds for parity (Furst–Saxe–Sipser). `[FSS84]`
- 1986: Håstad achieves near-optimal AC0 lower bounds. `[Has86]`
- 1992: IP = PSPACE (Shamir), a canonical non-relativizing result. `[Sha92]`
- 1994: Natural proofs barrier (Razborov–Rudich). `[RR94]`
- 1997: Quantum complexity theory framework (Bernstein–Vazirani). `[BV97]`
- 1997: Shor and Grover algorithms (journal versions). `[Sho97] [Gro97]`

## 2000s–2010s: refined barriers and new lower bounds
- 2009: Algebrization barrier (Aaronson–Wigderson, journal version). `[AW09]`
- 2009: Fortnow’s CACM synthesis on the status of P vs NP. `[For09]`
- 2011: Williams proves NEXP not in ACC0 (conference version). `[Wil11]`

## 2020s: quantum oracle separations (selected)
- 2022: Oracle separation of BQP and PH (Raz–Tal, JACM). `[RT22]`
